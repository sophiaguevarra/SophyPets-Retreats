Adoption Process

Step Description Timeframe
Browse Pets Look through our list of adoptable pets online or visit our shelter. Day 1 - Day 3
Submit Application Fill out and submit the adoption form with your details. Day 2 - Day 5
Home Visit / Interview Our team will contact you to schedule a short interview or home visit. Day 5 - Day 7
Meet Your Pet Visit the shelter to meet your chosen pet in person. Day 7 - Day 10
Adoption Day If approved, you can officially adopt and take home your new pet. Day 10 - Day 14
  • Browse Pets: Look through our list of adoptable pets online or visit our shelter.
  • Submit Application: Fill out and submit the adoption form with your details.
  • Home Visit / Interview: Our team will contact you to schedule a short interview or home visit.
  • Meet Your Pet: Visit the shelter to meet your chosen pet in person.
  • Adoption Day: If approved, you can officially adopt and take home your new pet.

Vet Support: Free first vet check-up after adoption.

Pet Starter Kit: Food, leash/collar, litter or bedding included.

Pet Care Guides: Tips for training, nutrition, and bonding.

Programs: Volunteer, foster, or donate to help more animals.
