About Us

SophyPets & Retreats founded in 2025, started with a simple abandoned and homeless pets. We began as a small group of volunteers rescuing stray animals that has grown into a community organization that has helped over 2,000 pets find their forever homes.

We believe adoption is more than just bringing a pet into your home; it’s about building lifelong companionship and love. Each adoption is guided by our core values of compassion, responsibility, and care.

Our Mission

  • Rescue & Rehabilitate: Save abandoned, neglected, and homeless pets, giving them proper care and medical attention.
  • Find Forever Homes: Match every rescued pet with a loving and responsible family.
  • Educate & Guide: Provide resources and guidance to help adopters become responsible pet parents.
  • Build a Community: Work with volunteers, donors, and partners to create a strong support system for animals in need.
  • Promote Compassion: Encourage kindness, respect, and responsible pet ownership in the community.
